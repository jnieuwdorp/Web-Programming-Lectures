Israeli politician and general Ariel Sharon (pictured) dies after being in a permanent vegetative state for eight years.

Archaeologists identify the tomb of Egyptian pharaoh Sobekhotep I.

The United States Senate confirms Janet Yellen as the Federal Reserve System's first female chairman.

The al-Qaeda-affiliated Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant captures the city of Fallujah.
